About Us

Natanaanjale School of Dance is the premiere institution for Bharatanatyam in the Greater Chennai area. The school’s founder, Smt. , Vimala Rama Narayanan is an acclaimed Bharatanatyam dancer who has dedicated her efforts to propagating this divine art-form to a diverse range of students.

Founded

Founded in 1992, Natanaanjale School of Dance has built a reputation of imparting rigorous training, while maintaining the rich cultural and spiritual traditions of Bharatanatyam. Natanaanjale ’s mission is to instill in students a love for Bharatanatyam, and to provide guidance and encouragement that supports the development of a true dancer’s grace, perfection, and perseverance.

Expertise

Students at Natanaanjale learn to perfect all facets of dancing: Bhava (expression), Raga (melody) and Tala (rhythm). Sridevi, with her assionate and signature teaching style brings out the best in her students both on and off the stage. Under Sridevi’s tutelage each student develops a life long work ethic that emphasizes a commitment to the pursuit of excellence in all aspects of their lives.
Many students have completed their Arangetram (dance debut) through the school, emerging as dancers who have won awards and acclaim in national and international performances that reflect Natanaanjale ’s mission. Natanaanjale School of Dance currently enrolls students from beginner to advanced levels.
